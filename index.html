Edgemont Robotics

Mission Statement:

Edgemont Robotics is a 7-12th grade STEAM focused team dedicated to celebrating collaboration, competition and creativity. The team was founded on the principle of a love of all things Robotics, which represented a coalition between future engineers, programmers and businessmen. Our team has also been incredibly resilient, and every year despite setbacks has continued to improve and get closer to our ultimate goal: making it to Worlds. As a culture, we celebrate Gracious Professionalism, and ensure that our environment is always incredibly open to positivity and curiosity.
