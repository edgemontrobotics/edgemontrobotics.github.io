Edgemont Robotics

Edging out the competition.

Who are we?

We are 7-12th grade STEAM focused team founded on the principle of a love for all things robotics. Our team has also been incredibly resilient, and every year despite setbacks has continued to improve and get closer to our ultimate goal: making it to Worlds. As a culture, we celebrate Gracious Professionalism, and ensure that our environment is always incredibly open to positivity and curiosity.

What do we do?

Every school year, as a part of the FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC), our team must design, build, program, and operate robots to compete in a head-to-head challenge in an alliance format. Click here to see the FTC challenge game for the 2021-22 season.

Get involved!

We are always looking for new members. Anyone can join, regardless of experience. Just drop in to one of our meetings either on Monday or Wednesday after school.
