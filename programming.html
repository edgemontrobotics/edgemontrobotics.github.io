Programming

Mission Statement

Programming is all about problem-solving and transferring skills developed in the classroom and in clubs and applying them to real world situations. Programming is no easy task, and requires hours upon hours of work and heavy collaboration with the mechanics team to make sure that the code written can be effectively applied and used. Programmers here alternate between working directly on tele-op, autonomous and on developing and upholding our entirely self-written website. We prioritize quick, short, efficient solutions as a philosophy when coding to make it more accessible from members from across the club to participate. This forces us to refine our strategy to the finest degree possible.
