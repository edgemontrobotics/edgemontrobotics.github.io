Outreach

What is outreach?

Outreach is all about community interaction and developing entrepreneurial and business skills. The Outreach team manages the treasury. We ensure the Robotics team has a strong balance to afford various expenses like parts and we work to contact various companies for sponsorships and fundraising events. Most importantly, the Outreach team works hard to promote Gracious Professionalism among all members and between all community interactions.
