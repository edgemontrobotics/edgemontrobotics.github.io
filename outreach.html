Outreach

Mission Statement:

The Outreach team at Edgemont Robotics is all about community interaction and developing entrepreneurial and business skills. Within outreach we work hard to help make Robotics welcoming to new members, and ensure that the Edgemont school community has a good relationship with us - through bake sales, collaborations with other clubs and even some support in directly involving ourselves with school budgeting: specifically development of the STEM department. In terms of fundraising, the Outreach team manages the treasury and all business activities. We ensure the Robotics team has a strong balance to afford various expenses like parts and work to contact various companies for sponsorships/fundraising events. Most importantly, the Outreach team works hard to promote Gracious Professionalism among all members and between all community interactions: we value hard-working committed efforts to reach out under absolute good faith and for the sake of fun.
